## Anchor Points

Anchor points are curated landmarks in a repository. They identify the places where behavior actually begins, changes, or crosses risk boundaries.

## Why They Matter

A payment retry loop and a route registration are not equally important. Anchor points tell the agent where to focus first.

## Knowledge Graph Layer

A graph built from anchors, files, dependencies, and communities helps the agent move beyond keyword search into relationship-aware exploration.

## Operational Payoff

With a graph, an agent can answer questions about blast radius, likely owners, connected systems, and change impact much faster.

## Summary

Anchor points compress human judgment. Knowledge graphs preserve structural relationships. Together they create reusable context for AI tooling.
