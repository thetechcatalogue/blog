## The Model Can Only Work With What It Sees

If the retrieved context is weak, stale, or noisy, the model starts from bad evidence.

## Prompting Is Not The Whole Story

Many failures blamed on prompting are really context failures. The instructions may be fine, but the wrong material reached the model.

## Good Context Is Filtered Context

Strong systems decide what to include, what to exclude, and how much evidence is worth carrying into the prompt.

## Quality Starts Before Generation

Search, retrieval, ranking, and prompt assembly shape the answer before the model writes a single token.

## Summary

Output quality often depends less on wording the prompt better and more on choosing context better.
