## Demos Hide The Real Failure Surface

A strong demo proves that one prompt worked once. It does not prove the system will hold up across real user traffic.

## The Core Problem

Teams often judge AI by intuition. If the answer sounds fluent, they assume it is good enough. That misses failures in correctness and consistency.

## Evals Create A Shared Standard

Evaluations make quality repeatable. Instead of debating impressions, the team can check whether the system passes the same tasks over time.

## Good Evals Measure The Right Thing

Depending on the product, that can mean groundedness, task completion, latency, format validity, safety, or citation quality.

## Why This Matters In Production

Without evals, regressions slip through quietly. Model, prompt, retrieval, or tool changes can all degrade behavior before anyone notices.

## Summary

Vibes help you notice a problem. Evals help you measure it and keep it from returning.
